\section*{Gamma spectra of all measured samples}
The following figures show the gamma spectra of the Benthos sample measured at the University of Alberta (figure \ref{AP:BC}) and the samples measured in the scope of this work: both small Vitrovex samples labelled as VV-1 (figure \ref{AP:VV1}) and VV-2 (figure \ref{AP:VV2}), the Vitrovex half pressure vessel (figure \ref{AP:VVes}), the Benthos half pressure vessel (figure \ref{AP:BVes}) and the gel samples Wacker SilGel 612 and QSI Qgel 900(figure \ref{AP:GEL}). Most of the identified peaks are labelled. The colour of the text corresponds to the decay chain of the isotope (red the \ch{^{232}Th}-series, blue \ch{^{238}U}- and yellow the \ch{^{235}U}-chain). The spectra of the optical gel samples do not differ from the background and therefore were illustrated in the same figure. The mass-specific activities for the isotopes summarised in table \ref{table:actCanada} and \ref{tab:actMue} (on page~\pageref{table:actCanada} and \pageref{tab:actMue}, respectively) were calculated with these spectra.
